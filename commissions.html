Ordering

The information I need when you order is; Necklace length, Color pallate, Metal color, If you want extra chains/strands, and a description of what you want the necklace to look like. I can send what pendants I have avalible if you would like one.

First, I will send you a preliminary sketch of what the necklace would look like and the bead mix i have made. This can be edited as many times as you like. Then I can move onto the layout on my bead board, I will send you an image and once that is confirmed I will make your necklace and ship it out.

Ordering

When you send me an email, Please include what you would like me to draw.

I will start with several thumbnail sketches. Once you choose the one you like, I will take my payment and start on the final sketch

I will check in to see if you like it and ask if you would like any changes.

Once you like it, I will do the lineart and flat color. I will check in one more time, at this point i can only do simple swaps such as color changes. Then I will render it.
